Conquistador

Conquistador Electronic Warfare System

Objective

To develop a tactical-level Electronic Warfare (EW) system.

Currently, Armed Forces of Ukraine are equipped with EW systems covering only 3% of the total requirement at the tactical level (platoon-company).

This is crucial for safeguarding personnel and equipment during combat missions against FPV drones, radio-controlled projectiles, UAVs, and radio-controlled mines.

Additionally, it serves to protect military, civilian, and critical infrastructure assets, thereby contributing to the defense industry's capabilities

Technical characteristics

  • Remote control 700-1100 MHz
  • Navigation GPS L1 + GLONASS 1575-1620 MHz
  • Remote radio control and video transmission 2400-2500 MHz 5150-5250 MHz 5700-5900 MHz
  • Capability to operate in complex field conditions, including on armored vehicles.
  • Convenient for transportation and quick installation.
  • Suppression radius of UAV control channels by directional antennas up to 3000 meters.
  • Waterproof
  • Protection according to the standards of the US Department of Defense MIL-STD 461/810.
  • Resistance to electromagnetic fields. DSTU EN 55014-2; DSTU EN 61000-6-14.
  • Equipment: Conquistador, transport cases, external battery, antennas, tripod.
